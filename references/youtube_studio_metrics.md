# YouTube Studio Metrics Mapping

## Required post-publish metrics

- CTR / impressions click-through rate.
- Intro retention after 30 seconds.
- Views.
- Views from impressions.
- Impressions.
- Average view duration.
- Traffic source.

## Derived metrics

```text
Packaging-to-30s conversion = CTR × Intro retention
Retained viewers per 1,000 impressions = CTR × Intro retention × 1000
30s retained views = views × intro retention
```

## Interpretation

| Pattern | Diagnosis | First action |
|---|---|---|
| High CTR + low intro retention | Packaging overpromises or intro under-delivers | Recut first 30s |
| Low CTR + high intro retention | Content is stronger than packaging | Rewrite title/thumbnail |
| Low CTR + low intro retention | Topic/package/opening all weak | Revalidate topic |
| High CTR + high intro retention | Good alignment | Make adjacent videos |

Use channel baselines when available. Avoid universal benchmark claims unless the user supplies their own historical comparison set.
