# Platform Rules

Created by **Cruz Olli**

Use this file to store platform-specific rules learned from measured performance.

## YouTube

### Hypotheses

- Direct food-tour wording may be safer than exaggerated claims for travel food videos.

### Working Rules

- Record rules only after repeated evidence across comparable videos.

### Metrics To Watch

- CTR
- First 30s retention
- Average view duration
- Subscribers gained per 1,000 views
- Browse versus search traffic

## Bilibili

### Hypotheses

- Fresh, natural covers may fit travel food vlogs better than heavy viral cover styling.

### Working Rules

- Record rules only after repeated evidence across comparable videos.

### Metrics To Watch

- Cover click rate if available
- Completion rate
- Follows gained
- Coins/favorites/comments
- Danmaku sentiment

## Shorts / Short-Form

### Hypotheses

- One clear food reaction per clip is easier to test than a broad travel montage.

### Working Rules

- Record rules only after repeated evidence across comparable clips.
